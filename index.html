I'm Micah Jeffries, a Hardware + Software Engineer and YouTuber.

I graduated magna cum laude from California Polytechnic State University in June 2022 majoring in Electrical Engineering and minoring in Computer Science.

I first launched my career in hardware engineering where I was part of a team responsible for designing enterprise-level networking switches. I now work in the water/wastewater industry devloping software user interfaces for industrial equipment.

I recently launched my YouTube Channel in the hopes of providing support and guidance to students who struggle to make one of the most important decisions of their life: choosing a field of study.

As an engineer who has been working in the industry for 3+ years now, I initially had no idea what I was getting myself into by signing up for engineering school. A problem that I have found to be quite common among many aspiring college students. My content is provided to help young students make an informed decision by knowing what you can expect to learn and what types of careers are out there.
