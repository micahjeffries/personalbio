Research

A collection of published and unpublished academic works.

8 research papers

Handwritten Digit Recognition

Convolutional neural networks (CNN) are among the most predominant technology for image pattern recognition. This paper documents the design and experimental results of a handwritten digit recognition system based on a CNN with ProMoD backpropogation.

Loaded Circular Loop Antenna

The cardiod single-lobed pattern is desirable for its ability to eliminate noise interference from backward reflecting waves in a strong signal environment. This paper documents the design and experimental results of such a two-port loaded circular antenna.

Landslide Prediction

This recommendation report analyzes three possible methods for predicting landslides and evaulates each option based on accuracy, reliability, availability, and cost-efficiency.

Parking Lot Detection

This paper proposes two solutions for detecting vacant parking lot spaces using computer vision techniques.

Analog Processor with Time-Encoded Computation

This paper proposes a design of an analog processing element using time-encoded computation. Time-encoded computation leverages the strangths of analog circuits, namely low power consumption and high information density, while also avoiding its weaknesses such as hardware design difficulty, low precision, limited scalability and programming difficulty.

Second Order Modulator

This paper documents the design and experimental results of a second order modulator. The modulator accepts an analog input signal and produces a PWM-like digital signal with an average voltage equivalent to the input.

Ultrasonic Sensor

This paper documents the design and experimental results of an ultrasonic sensor which is an electroni device that utlizes a transmitter and receiver to measure the distance of a target object.

Three Output Noise Generator

This paper documents the design and experimental results of an analog-based processor that outputs three different types of noise.
